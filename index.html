Heatwave Impact on European Electricity Grids

Understanding climate change effects on power infrastructure

Learn More About Heatwaves

Overview

This study examines the effects of heatwaves on European electricity grids. As climate change intensifies, understanding these impacts becomes crucial for enhancing societal security and resilience. The research focuses on analyzing how increasing frequency, intensity, and duration of heatwaves affect electricity grid operations and reliability.

European air temperature anomalies (source: climate.copernicus.eu)

Methodology

Future Heatwave Projection

Generates projected heatwave events (2025-2050) based on historical data from 2019 and 2022.

Future Demand Modeling

Utilizes Demand.ninja for weather-dependent power demand modeling.

Renewable Generation

Calculates renewable generation using Atlite framework.

Conductor Thermal Modeling

Analyzes temperature effects on conductor properties and thermal limits.

Multi-Segment Modeling

Identifies localized stress points and potential bottlenecks.

Optimal Power Flow Analysis

Simulates grid response under thermal and demand stresses.
